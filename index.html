Welcome to SF Paws Adoption

SF Paws Adoption is a community-based, nonprofit organization dedicated to finding loving homes for dogs and cats throughout San Francisco. Since our founding, we have partnered with local shelters and volunteers to rescue animals in need and connect them with families who will care for them for life.

Our Mission

We believe every animal deserves a safe, loving home. Our mission is to reduce pet homelessness in San Francisco through adoption, community education, and low-cost spay and neuter programs. We work closely with local veterinarians and foster families to ensure every animal in our care is healthy and ready for adoption.

How Adoption Works

Adopting a pet from SF Paws Adoption is simple. Browse our list of adoptable pets, fill out an application, and meet your potential new companion at one of our scheduled adoption events. Our team will guide you through every step of the process, from application to bringing your new pet home.

Why Adopt?

  • Save a life and open up space for another animal in need
  • Support a local nonprofit organization
  • All pets are vaccinated, spayed or neutered, and microchipped
  • Ongoing support and resources for new pet owners

Visit Us

SF Paws Adoption is located in San Francisco, CA. Our adoption center is open Tuesday through Sunday, 10am to 5pm. Walk-ins are welcome, but we recommend scheduling an appointment to meet a specific animal.
